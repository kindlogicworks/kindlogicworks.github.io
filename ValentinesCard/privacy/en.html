Privacy Policy

Valentines Card
Effective date: 03/06/2026

1. Introduction

Valentines Card ("we", "our", or "the App"), published by Kindlogic Works, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App may collect, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Greeting cards created by you
  • Photos selected by you for card creation
  • App preferences and settings
  • Saved designs and editing data

This information is stored locally on your device and is not sent to our servers.

3. Advertising

The App may use Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • App interaction information
  • Diagnostic information

This information is collected and processed by Google according to Google's privacy policies.

4. Analytics and Diagnostics

The App may use analytics and diagnostic services, such as Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, to help us improve the App and provide a better user experience.

Analytics and diagnostic data may include:

  • App opens
  • Screens viewed
  • General app interactions
  • Feature usage events
  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports

We do not use analytics data to identify individual users.

5. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

The App does not intentionally collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Banking information
  • GPS location
  • Contacts
  • Personal identification information

6. How We Use Information

Information may be used to:

  • Create and edit greeting cards
  • Save your designs locally
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Understand overall app usage trends

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Anonymous or aggregated analytics, diagnostic, and advertising-related data may be processed by third-party services such as Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics according to their own privacy policies.

8. User Control

You can remove locally stored app data by:

  • Deleting saved content within the App, where available
  • Clearing the App's data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App

9. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
