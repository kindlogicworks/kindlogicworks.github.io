Privacy Policy

Should I Buy This?

Effective date: 30/04/2026

1. Introduction

Should I Buy This? (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App may collect, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Income settings
  • Work hours and workdays
  • Product prices entered by you
  • Purchase decision history
  • App preferences such as language, currency, and theme

This information is stored locally on your device and is not sent to our servers.

3. Analytics Data

The App may use analytics services, such as Google Firebase Analytics, to help us understand how the App is used and improve the user experience.

Analytics data may include:

  • App opens
  • Screens viewed
  • General app interactions
  • Feature usage events

We do not send personal purchase details, product names, prices, income values, notes, or decision history content to analytics.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not intentionally collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Banking information
  • GPS location
  • Contacts
  • Personal financial account information

5. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Generate purchase decision results
  • Save your local decision history
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve app performance and usability

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

Anonymous or aggregated analytics data may be processed by third-party services such as Google Firebase Analytics according to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data. Most app data is stored locally on your device. Analytics data, when used, is transmitted through secure connections provided by the analytics service.

8. User Control

You can delete app data by:

  • Clearing history inside the App, where available
  • Clearing the App’s data from your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
